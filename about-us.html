About SEACET

South East Asian Education Trust was established in 1972 under the leadership of Sri A. Krishnappa to provide quality education. SEACET, located in Bangalore, is committed to holistic development and technical excellence.

Vision

To be a global leader in technical education, fostering innovation, research, and ethical values.

Mission

  • Provide transformative education to empower students.
  • Promote research and industry collaboration.
  • Develop socially responsible engineers.

History

Founded by Sri A. Krishnappa, SEACET evolved from schools to a premier engineering institution, serving thousands of students over decades.

Leadership

Chairman, Sri Manjunath A. Gowda: "Our goal is to create leaders who innovate and inspire."

Secretary, Sri Shivaprasad A. Gowda: "We focus on quality education and student welfare."

Principal, Dr. K. R. Paramahamsa: "SEACET is dedicated to academic excellence and research."
